Taj Mahal

Nearest Metro:Taj Mahal Metro Station

Address:Dharmapuri, Forest Colony, Tajganj, Agra, Uttar Predesh

It was built by the Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan as a memorial for his third wife, Mumtaz Mahal.Constructed entirely out of white marble in the 17th century,it is among the finest edifices of Mughl architecture. Recognised by the UNESCO as a world heritage site,this monument is also considerd to bee one of the seven wonders of the modern world.

Agra Fort

Nearest Metro:Agra Fort Metro Station

Address:Agra Fort,Rakabganj, Agra, Uttar Predesh

Agra Fort is one of the most special monuments built byy the Mughals and has richly decorated buildings. It is the perfect example of the Mughal style of art and architecture and is one of the most visited tourist spots in Agra. Built purely out of red sandstone, this expansive architecture stands out against the soft azure of the sky. Within the premises of the fort lie the most exquisite stuctures of the Mughals like the Pearl Mosque, Diwan-i l khas, Diwan-i l Aam, Moti Masjid and Jahangiri Mahal.

Itimad-ud-daulah's Tomb

Nearest Metro:Fatehabad Road Metro Station

Address:Moti Bagh, Agra, Uttar Predesh

The tomb of Itimad-ud-daulah marks the transition from the first phase of the Mughal architecture to the second. This was the first structure to make use of pietra dura and the first to be built on the banks of the Yamuna Rivet. It basically consists of Indo-Oslamic architecture,with the use of arched entrances and octagonal shaped towers.

Mehtab Bagh

Nearest Metro:Taj Mahal Metro Station

Address:Taj Mahal, Dharmapuri, Forest Colony, Nagla Devjit, Agra, Uttar Predesh

The splendid Mehtab Bagh has four sandstone towers, one each at the corner with a huge octagonal tank right in the centre. It is believed that Shah Jahan especially got the bagh designed for his personal interests so he could come up here and admire beauty of his favourite piece of art.

Jama Masjid

Nearest Metro:Jama Masjid Metro Station

Address:Jama Masjid Road, Subash Bazar, Kinari Bazar,Hing ki Mandi, Mantola, Agra, Uttar Predesh

The Jama Masjid is build mainly of red sandstone and white marble that stand as a testimony to the magnificrnt Mughal arcitecture. The design of jama masjid is unique.It has a fountain at the centre and is supported by four kiosks in the courtyard. The interior walls have scripures in the Persian style praising for Shah Jahan and Jahan Ara.

Chini ka Rauza

Nearest Metro:Ram Bagh Metro Station

Address:Katra Wazir Khan, Agra, Uttar Predesh

The monument located 1 km north of Itimad-ud-daulah's Tomb, is hidden away in an alcove of trees and is usually an unusual but a happy discovery among tourists. It is supposedly the first of its kind to be decorated by glazed tile work.

Wildlife SOS

Nearest Metro:Sikandara Metro Station

Address:Inde, NH2, Muranda, Uttar Predesh

Wildlife SOS is most popularly known for their applaudable work of saving around 600 'dancing bears' from the tribal people. Not until long ago and sometimes even now, the tribals trap bears and train them to dance for the people as a mode of street entertainment, in return for few bucks.

Anguri Bagh

Nearest Metro:Agra Fort Metro Station

Address:Agra Fort, Rakabganj, Agra, Uttar Predesh

This sprawling charbagh (four complex compartment) styled garden is flecked with intricate geometrical patterns. Originally popular for thick creepers of grapes-their cultivation and harvesting,the garden accurately came to be called as the garden of Grapes (Anguri Bagh).

Taj museum

Nearest Metro:Taj Mahal Metro Station

Address:Taj Mahal, Dharmapuri, Forest Colony, Tajganj, Agra, Uttar Predesh

As the name suggests, the museum is all about telling the tale of the Taj. It is a popular place of visit among the travellers as it is home to the facts and history relating to the magnificent monument. You can also find gold and silver coins minted in Agra at the time. We recommend you to visit the place if you are in for a little historical and factual retreat.
